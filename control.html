Common questions.

Does this work with our CRM?

CONTROL is built to work inside your existing CRM — HubSpot, Pipedrive, Planhat, and others. We assess your setup in week one and build accordingly. No migration required.

How much time does this take from our team?

Typically 2–3 hours per week from one internal stakeholder during the pilot. We do the build. Your team reviews, gives feedback, and takes over at handover.

What happens after the pilot?

You own it. Full documentation, clean handover, your team runs it. If you want to extend or add a second pilot, we can — but there's no dependency on us to keep it running.

We only have a small CS team. Is this still relevant?

Especially then. A small team can't monitor every account manually. CONTROL makes sure the right accounts surface automatically — so your CSMs spend time where it matters.
