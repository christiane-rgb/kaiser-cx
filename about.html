About Kaiser CX

Shaped by experience, not theory.

CONTROL came from years of watching CS teams work hard — on the wrong accounts, at the wrong time, without the systems to know the difference.

The story

Why CONTROL exists.

When gut feel stops working and churn starts appearing in the forecast, you need structure that actually holds. Not another dashboard. Not a new tool. A system — built into the way your team already works.

Christiane Kaiser spent 15+ years building and scaling CS organizations in fast-growing B2B environments. What kept showing up: the problem was never the team. It was the missing logic. No clear signal for when to act. No automated path from insight to action. CSMs reacting instead of steering.

CONTROL is the answer to that. Every business has different levers, different customers, different breaking points. CONTROL works because it's built around yours — your segmentation, your thresholds, your playbooks.

What's repeatable gets automated. What needs judgment stays with your team. The logic is yours. The system makes it scale.

Christiane works directly with founders and CS leaders — not through layers of consultants, not with a generic methodology. The starting point is always how you actually operate. The build follows from there.

The result: CS capacity scales without headcount. Churn gets flagged early enough to act. Expansion stops being left to chance.
